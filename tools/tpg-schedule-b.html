IAR Agreement · Schedule B Amendment · Confidential

Schedule B — Platform Services
Amendment & Clarification

This document amends and restates Schedule B of the IAR Agreement between ThePARTNERS and each Salvus IAR, incorporating revisions negotiated by John Suender and confirmed by Gregory Rutherford. This Schedule B supersedes any prior version and is incorporated into the IAR Agreement in its entirety.

Preamble

Platform Services Provided by ThePARTNERS

In exchange for the Platform Fee of seven percent (7%) of gross advisory revenue retained by ThePARTNERS pursuant to the Business Management Agreement, ThePARTNERS shall provide the following platform services to each IAR engaged under the IAR Agreement (collectively, the "Platform Services"):

Investment Platform
Access to and use of Amplify, ThePARTNERS' primary investment management and reporting platform (the "on-platform" system), including all standard features, reporting modules, and updates made generally available to ThePARTNERS IARs.
Technology & Systems
Microsoft 365 licenses including ShareFile for document management and secure client communications. Additional tools and services as ThePARTNERS may add from time to time with reasonable advance notice to IARs.
IT Support
IT support services provided through Myriad. For the avoidance of doubt, such services shall not include the provision, purchase, reimbursement, or replacement of computers, mobile phones, or other hardware devices. (Confirmed by Gregory Rutherford, June 11, 2026.)
Compliance Services
Full compliance services for IARs, including ongoing supervision, compliance monitoring, review of client communications and marketing materials, and maintenance of ThePARTNERS' compliance program in accordance with the Investment Advisers Act of 1940 and applicable regulations.
Insurance Coverage
Errors and omissions (E&O) insurance coverage and other liability coverage, including cyber liability and theft coverage, provided at ThePARTNERS' standard coverage levels and based on ThePARTNERS' normal practices. Important limitation: If an IAR's individual business activities require expansion of insurance coverage beyond ThePARTNERS' standard program, ThePARTNERS reserves the right to invoice such IAR for the incremental cost of such expanded coverage, subject to prior written notice to the IAR and the IAR's written approval of the additional cost. (Confirmed by Gregory Rutherford, June 11, 2026.)
Licensing Fees
Federal and state licensing fees and regulatory filing costs as follows: ThePARTNERS will cover all licensing fees and costs associated with advisor registration and regulatory filings — including the filing and maintenance of each IAR's Form U4 — in the three (3) states with the highest number of that IAR's client accounts as of each annual renewal date. Additional state registrations beyond those three states are the responsibility of the IAR, subject to prior written agreement between the IAR and ThePARTNERS. (Modified per negotiation; supersedes prior language referencing unlimited state coverage.)
Custody
Custody services primarily through Schwab Institutional and Fidelity Investments, with support of other custodial relationships as required. Additional custodial fees from third-party custodians may apply and shall be disclosed to the IAR in advance.
Investment Resources
Fully vetted and compliance-approved model managers, portfolio managers, strategists, and third-party money managers available on ThePARTNERS' approved third-party platforms, including trading and rebalancing services. ThePARTNERS' Chief Investment Officer shall provide due diligence and ongoing supervision of approved managers.
Business Development
Marketing and business development assistance, including periodic client thought pieces created by or for ThePARTNERS, and marketing materials reviewed and approved for compliance. Assistance is provided on a best-efforts basis and does not constitute a guaranteed deliverable or minimum service level.
Succession Planning
Succession planning assistance on a best-efforts basis. ThePARTNERS will work with IARs to develop succession plans consistent with regulatory requirements and ThePARTNERS' platform capabilities.
Training
Ongoing training on platform systems, compliance requirements, and regulatory developments, provided through ThePARTNERS' standard training programs made available to all IARs.
Clarification

Scope Limitations

Services Not Included

The Platform Services described above do not include the following, which remain the responsibility of the IAR or Salvus Wealth Management LLC as the employer of record:

(a) Hardware devices including computers, mobile phones, tablets, or other physical equipment;

(b) State licensing fees for states beyond the three highest-client-count states per IAR per annual renewal period, unless separately agreed in writing;

(c) Incremental insurance premiums arising from an individual IAR's business activities beyond ThePARTNERS' standard coverage program, subject to prior notice and IAR approval;

(d) Custodial fees charged directly by Schwab Institutional, Fidelity, or other third-party custodians beyond ThePARTNERS' standard custodial arrangement;

(e) Third-party software, subscriptions, or services not expressly listed above.
