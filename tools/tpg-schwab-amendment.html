Background

Purpose of This Amendment

ThePARTNERS maintains a referral arrangement with Charles Schwab Institutional ("Schwab") pursuant to which ThePARTNERS pays Schwab a referral fee based on a percentage of average daily assets under management in client accounts introduced or attributable to ThePARTNERS ("Schwab Referral Fee"). The Schwab Referral Fee is deducted from IAR compensation pursuant to the Compensation Schedule (Exhibit A). This amendment establishes a clear, predictable methodology for estimating, holding back, paying, and reconciling the Schwab Referral Fee in connection with Robert O'Quinn's client accounts.

Section 1

Schwab Referral Fee Calculation & Holdback

1.1 Standard Quarterly Periods

For each standard calendar quarter billing period, ThePARTNERS shall estimate the Schwab Referral Fee attributable to IAR's client accounts as follows:

Estimated Schwab Referral Fee = 0.22% × Average Daily AUM for the billing period ÷ 4

where "Average Daily AUM" means the average of the daily ending market values of all IAR client accounts held at Schwab during the applicable billing period, as calculated from Schwab's records or ThePARTNERS' reporting systems.

1.2 Non-Standard or Stub Billing Periods

For any billing period that does not correspond to a full calendar quarter — including initial stub periods arising from client onboarding across multiple dates — the Schwab Referral Fee shall be estimated as follows:

Estimated Schwab Referral Fee = 0.22% × Average Daily AUM for the actual billing period × (number of calendar days in billing period ÷ 365)

For the avoidance of doubt, the calculation methodology for any non-standard billing period must be agreed upon in writing by both ThePARTNERS and IAR before billing is processed. ThePARTNERS shall provide IAR with the proposed calculation methodology and estimated holdback amount no less than five (5) business days before billing is initiated, and IAR shall confirm or raise objections within two (2) business days.

Section 2

Payment Timing

2.1 Payment of Balance After Holdback

Upon completion of billing for each period, ThePARTNERS shall:

(a) Calculate the estimated Schwab Referral Fee holdback pursuant to Section 1;

(b) Deduct the estimated holdback from the total advisory fees collected;

(c) Pay the remaining balance to IAR within two (2) business days of billing completion, directing payment to the bank account of Salvus Wealth Management LLC pursuant to Section 3.5 of the IAR Agreement.

2.2 Receipt of Schwab Invoice

Schwab typically delivers its invoice to ThePARTNERS within fourteen (14) calendar days following the billing period end date. Upon receipt of Schwab's actual invoice, ThePARTNERS shall:

(a) Compare the actual Schwab Referral Fee invoiced against the estimated holdback;

(b) Calculate any variance (positive or negative) between the estimate and the actual;

(c) Pay any surplus holdback to IAR, or invoice IAR for any shortfall, within two (2) business days of Schwab invoice receipt.

2.3 Disputed Schwab Invoices

If ThePARTNERS disputes the amount of a Schwab invoice, ThePARTNERS shall:

(a) Notify IAR in writing within two (2) business days of receiving the disputed invoice;

(b) Pay IAR any portion of the holdback that is not subject to dispute within two (2) business days of the dispute notification;

(c) Resolve the dispute with Schwab and pay any remaining holdback balance to IAR within five (5) business days of resolution.

Undisputed amount rule: Under no circumstances shall a dispute between ThePARTNERS and Schwab regarding the Schwab Referral Fee amount cause ThePARTNERS to withhold the uncontested portion of IAR's compensation. The uncontested balance must be paid within the timelines above regardless of any pending dispute with Schwab.
2.4 Maximum Holdback Period

In no event shall any portion of the estimated Schwab Referral Fee holdback be retained by ThePARTNERS for more than thirty (30) calendar days from the date of billing completion, unless IAR has provided written consent to an extended holdback period for a specific billing cycle.

Section 3

Transparency & Reporting

3.1 Billing Statement

With each quarterly payment, ThePARTNERS shall provide IAR with a written billing statement setting forth:

(a) Total gross advisory fees collected from IAR's client accounts for the period;

(b) The estimated Schwab Referral Fee holdback amount and the calculation methodology used;

(c) Any other deductions applied pursuant to the Compensation Schedule;

(d) The net amount paid to IAR; and

(e) The account-level detail supporting the AUM used in the Schwab Referral Fee calculation.

3.2 Reconciliation Statement

Upon receipt of the Schwab invoice and completion of reconciliation under Section 2.2, ThePARTNERS shall provide IAR with a reconciliation statement showing the actual Schwab Referral Fee, the estimated holdback, the variance, and the resulting payment or credit to IAR.

3.3 Annual Review

The parties shall review the Schwab referral rate (currently 0.22% per annum) annually. If Schwab modifies its referral fee rate, ThePARTNERS shall notify IAR in writing within ten (10) business days of receiving notice from Schwab, and this amendment shall be updated to reflect the revised rate for subsequent billing periods.

Execution
